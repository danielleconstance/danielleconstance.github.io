Bio

Latay is fresh to the Miami music scene, Latay’s love of Hip Hop, R&B and EDM inspired her to bring her new “brew” style to the DJ world. Hailing from New York she grew up with a variety of influences from old school Rhythm and Blues, Funk to Reggae. As she became an adamant member of the festival scene it opened her eyes to the mega DJs existing in the EDM realm and the powerful impact of music. Join her on her journey through complex beats and genre exploring tracks. Latay’s music is piping hot and will have you coming back for more.
